Investigating the genetic landscape of Alzheimer's disease and related dementias in the context of hypertension 

This is the online repository for the manuscript titled " Investigating the genetic landscape of Alzheimer's disease and related dementias in the context of hypertension". This study represents the first and largest genome-wide assessment (GWAS) of Alzheimer’s disease and related dementia in context of hypertension. The discovery database used for the study was “UK Biobank” and the replication database was “All of Us”. Further, meta-analysis and post analysis was done combining both the datasets.
